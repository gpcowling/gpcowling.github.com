Nominations Close in Branch Elections

Feb 8th, 2013

Nominations were requested in January to fill positions in our annual branch elections, and to elect delegates from our branch to the PCS Conference in May. Nominations have now closed, and all positions were filled uncontested. We would like to welcome all newcomers to the branch. You can see a full list of your reps on the About page.
