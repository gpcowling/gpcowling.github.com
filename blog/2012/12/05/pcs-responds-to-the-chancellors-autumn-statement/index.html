PCS Responds to the Chancellor's Autumn Statement

Dec 5th, 2012

This afternoon’s statement from George Osborne to the House of Commons was met with a strong response from the PCS general secretary Mark Serwotka:

“It is glaringly obvious that not only is the millionaire chancellor George Osborne wildly out of touch with the lives of millions of people in this country, but also that his economic plans are miles off course.”

For more information on the union’s campaigns against the cuts you can visit the PCS website at www.pcs.org.uk.
